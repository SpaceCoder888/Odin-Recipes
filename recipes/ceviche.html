Peruvian Ceviche Recipe

One of the most delicious dishes from South America

There are many different kinds of ceviche, Peruvian Ceviche is the one I grew up eating. Ceviche is a delicious meal that is easily prepared with several ingredients. In order to cook Ceviche you need potatoes, red onions, lime juice, stalk celery, cilantro leaves, ground cumin, garlic, habanero pepper, ground pepper, fresh tilapia, shrimp, and lettuce. Those are all the things that you need in order to cook Ceviche. It's very simple to cook and once you are finished preparing it the meal tastes delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 2 normal potatoes
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 sliced stalk celery
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves
  • 1 pinch ground cumin
  • 1 minced clove garlic
  • 1 habanero pepper
  • ground pepper
  • 1 pound fresh tilapia, cut up
  • 1 pound medium shrimp, peeled
  • boston lettuce

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Place all of the potatoes in a saucepan covered with water, drain the potatoes after they're easily stabbed with a fork, and let them cool off.
  2. Put the sliced onion into a bowl of warm water, leave it there for 10 minutes, then drain it, leave it alone.
  3. Blend the lime juice, cilantro, celery and cumin all together. Pour into a bowl and stir the habanero pepper and the garlic into it.
  4. Add salt and pepper to the mix and stire the shrimp and the diced tilapia into it.
  5. Let it chill out for 60 minutes, stir it every 10 minutes, once it turns firm the seafood is done.
  6. Peel the potatoes and slice them up. Put the onions into the fish, put lettuce leaves into the plates of food, pour the ceviche with all its juice into the bowls.
