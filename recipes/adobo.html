Filipino Chicken Adobo

One Of The Most Exquisite Dishes of the Philippines

The Philippines is a beautiful country of over 7,000 tropical islands, not only does it have many islands, it also has many dishes to enjoy. One of the meals of the philippines is the Filipino Chicken Adobo. It is a simple, easily cooked meal that tastes great, it's perfect for when you invite guests over to your home.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and sliced
  • 3 cloves fresh garlic, minced
  • 1 pound chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorns
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon fine salt, or to taste

Recipe Instructions

  1. Combine the soy sauce, jalapeno peppers, garlic and chicken wings in a bowl together.
  2. Place plastic wrap over the bowl, set the bowl into the fridge for 30 minutes.
  3. Remove the chicken wings from the bowl.
  4. Heat up oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
  5. Place the chicken wings into the oil and fry for 2 minutes in a pan.
  6. Pour in the marinade that was left aside in the bowl.
  7. Add bay leaves and peppercorn.
  8. Place a read thermometer near the bone, it should say 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
  9. Stir in vinegar, add sugar and salt on it, cook it for 5 minutes.
